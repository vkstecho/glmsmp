Why boats fail early — and what to track
नाव जल्दी क्यों खराब होती है
FreeVideo 4 Cards3 Quiz
Boats are the most expensive consumable on a metalliser. Understanding why they fail — and how to track your consumption correctly — can save your plant thousands of rupees per month.

नाव मेटलाइज़र पर सबसे महंगी उपभोग्य वस्तु हैं।
Key Points

🔥 Too high OD = boat stress

Running above target OD increases thermal stress on the boat, causing early failure. Keep OD in range.

💧 Contamination

Moisture or oil on the film surface causes violent evaporation that cracks boats prematurely.

✓ Track per roll

Log boats used per roll, not per shift. This shows you exactly which rolls are consuming more boats.

⚠ Web break = wasted boat

Every web break wastes the remaining life of the current boat. Reducing web breaks directly reduces boat use.

What is the main cause of boat failure at high OD values?
✓ Correct! Thermal stress from excessive OD is the #1 cause of early boat failure.
